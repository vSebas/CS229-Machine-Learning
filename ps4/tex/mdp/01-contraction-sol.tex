Let $f(a_f) = \max_a f(a)$, and $g(a_g) = \max_a g(a)$.

Thus,
\[
\max_a f(a) - \max_a g(a) = f(a_f) - g(a_g)
\]

Since $f(a_f) \geq f(a_g)$,
\[
f(a_f) - g(a_g) \leq f(a_g) - g(a_g)
\]

\begin{align*}
B(V_1) - B(V_2)

 - \gamma \max_{a\in A} \sum_{s'\in S} P_{sa}(s')V_2(s') \\
&=  \gamma \left[ \max_{a\in A} \sum_{s'\in S} P_{sa}(s') V_1(s') - \max_{a\in A} \sum_{s'\in S} P_{sa}(s')V_2(s') \right] \\
\end{align*}
\]

f(a) = \sum_{s' \in S} P_{sa}(s')V_1(s'), \qquad f(a) = \sum_{s' \in S} P_{sa}(s')V_2(s')
\]
